# JLPT N3 Comprehensive Study Plan (12 Weeks)

## WEEKS 1-4: FOUNDATION BUILDING

### WEEK 1: Conditional Forms

#### Monday: Grammar
- **Material**: Shin Kanzen Master N3 Grammar, Unit 1 (pp. 10-13)
- **Focus**: ～ば and ～たら conditionals
- **Activity**: Read explanations, complete provided exercises 1-3
- **Homework**: Create 5 original sentences using each form

#### Tuesday: Kanji
- **Material**: Shin Kanzen Master N3 Kanji, Section 1
- **Focus**: First 15 N3 kanji (focus on everyday terms)
- **Activity**: Write each kanji 5 times, create vocabulary flashcards
- **Review**: Monday's grammar points with 3 example sentences each

#### Wednesday: Grammar & Vocabulary
- **Material**: Shin Kanzen Master N3 Grammar, Unit 1 (pp. 14-17)
- **Focus**: ～と and ～なら conditionals
- **Activity**: Complete provided exercises 4-6
- **Vocabulary**: Shin Kanzen Master N3 Vocabulary, Group 1 (25 words)

#### Thursday: Reading & Grammar Application
- **Material**: Bunpro (search for passages using conditional forms)
- **Focus**: Identifying conditional forms in context
- **Activity**: Read passages, answer comprehension questions
- **Review**: Week's grammar points and vocabulary

#### Friday: Review & Practice
- **Material**: Previous JLPT N3 tests (grammar section only)
- **Focus**: Conditional forms in test format
- **Activity**: Timed practice (25 minutes), review incorrect answers

## Vocabulary Study Integration

Throughout the 12 weeks, vocabulary study should closely follow the grammar points:

- **Week 1-4**: 100 words (25/week) from Shin Kanzen Master N3 Vocabulary Groups 1-4
- **Week 5-8**: 120 words (30/week) from Shin Kanzen Master N3 Vocabulary Groups 5-8
- **Week 9-12**: 160 words (40/week) from Shin Kanzen Master N3 Vocabulary Groups 9-12 plus review

## Kanji Study Integration

- **Week 1-4**: 60 kanji (15/week) alternating between Shin Kanzen Master N3 Kanji and WaniKani
- **Week 5-8**: 80 kanji (20/week) alternating between Shin Kanzen Master N3 Kanji and WaniKani
- **Week 9-12**: Comprehensive review of all 140 kanji plus remaining N3 kanji as needed
